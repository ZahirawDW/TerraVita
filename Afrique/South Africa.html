South Africa in Summer

Experience the diverse landscapes, wildlife, and culture of South Africa!

Information collected by TerraVita's team

Cape Town

Enjoy stunning views of the ocean and Table Mountain.

  • Activities: Hiking, sightseeing, wine tasting, visiting Robben Island.
  • Highlights: Table Mountain, V&A Waterfront, Boulders Beach (penguins).

Kruger National Park

Discover Africa's wildlife in one of the largest game reserves.

  • Activities: Safari tours, wildlife photography, birdwatching.
  • Highlights: Big Five animals, luxury lodges, guided safaris.

Johannesburg

Explore the vibrant city known for its history and culture.

  • Activities: Sightseeing, visiting museums, shopping, nightlife.
  • Highlights: Apartheid Museum, Soweto, Gold Reef City.

Durban

Relax on the beautiful beaches and explore the vibrant coastal city.

  • Activities: Surfing, beach activities, cultural tours, shopping.
  • Highlights: Golden Mile, uShaka Marine World, Victoria Street Market.
