Seychelles in Summer

Experience the beauty of Seychelles with its pristine beaches and tropical activities!

Information collected by TerraVita's team

Anse Source d'Argent

Relax on one of the most famous beaches in the world, with turquoise waters and white sand.

  • Activities: Sunbathing, swimming, snorkeling, photography.
  • Highlights: Beautiful granite rocks, clear waters, and lush surroundings.

Praslin Island

Explore the natural beauty of Praslin, home to the unique Vallée de Mai National Park.

  • Activities: Hiking, wildlife watching, visiting local villages.
  • Highlights: Vallée de Mai, Coco de Mer, and secluded beaches.

Mahe Island

Discover the main island of Seychelles, known for its vibrant culture and beautiful beaches.

  • Activities: Beach hopping, visiting local markets, hiking in the mountains.
  • Highlights: Beau Vallon beach, Morne Seychellois National Park, and vibrant markets.

Curieuse Island

A protected island known for its giant tortoises and mangrove forests.

  • Activities: Nature walks, wildlife encounters, snorkeling.
  • Highlights: Giant tortoises, marine life, and untouched natural beauty.
