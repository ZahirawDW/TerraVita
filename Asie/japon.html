Japan in Spring

Experience the beauty of Japan in spring, with cherry blossoms and stunning landscapes!

Tokyo

Explore the vibrant capital with its mix of modern and traditional attractions.

  • Activities: Visit the famous Shibuya Crossing, Meiji Shrine, and Ueno Park.
  • Highlights: Cherry blossoms in parks, modern skyscrapers, and cultural heritage.

Kyoto

Experience the traditional beauty of Japan with its ancient temples and gardens.

  • Activities: Visit the Golden Pavilion, Fushimi Inari Shrine, and Arashiyama Bamboo Grove.
  • Highlights: Tranquil gardens, beautiful temples, and historic districts.

Mount Fuji

Admire the iconic symbol of Japan surrounded by beautiful spring flowers.

  • Activities: Hiking, sightseeing, and photography.
  • Highlights: Stunning views, climbing Mount Fuji, and the surrounding lakes.

Nara

Visit the historic city known for its friendly deer and beautiful parks.

  • Activities: Explore Nara Park, Todai-ji Temple, and Kasuga Taisha Shrine.
  • Highlights: Peaceful parks, ancient temples, and the famous Nara deer.
