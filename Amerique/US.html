United States in Spring

Discover the best places to visit in the United States during the spring season!

Information collected by TerraVita's team

New York City

Explore the iconic landmarks and vibrant culture of the Big Apple during springtime.

  • Activities: Visiting Central Park, seeing Broadway shows, and exploring museums.
  • Highlights: The Statue of Liberty, Times Square, and the Empire State Building.

Washington, D.C.

Witness the stunning cherry blossoms and explore the nation's capital.

  • Activities: Visiting the National Mall, touring the museums, and viewing the cherry blossoms.
  • Highlights: The White House, Lincoln Memorial, and Smithsonian museums.

San Francisco

Enjoy the picturesque landscapes and iconic attractions of this California city in spring.

  • Activities: Walking across the Golden Gate Bridge, visiting Alcatraz Island, and enjoying Fisherman's Wharf.
  • Highlights: Golden Gate Bridge, Alcatraz, and the colorful Painted Ladies.

Grand Canyon

Experience the breathtaking views of the Grand Canyon with fewer crowds in the spring season.

  • Activities: Hiking, rafting, and scenic flights.
  • Highlights: Grand Canyon views, sunset over the canyon, and hiking trails.
