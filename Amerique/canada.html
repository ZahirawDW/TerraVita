Canada in Winter

Discover the best places and activities to enjoy in Canada during the winter season!

Information collected by TerraVita's team

Banff National Park

Experience the stunning mountain landscapes and winter sports.

  • Activities: Skiing, snowboarding, winter hiking, wildlife viewing.
  • Highlights: Snow-covered peaks, frozen lakes, and cozy mountain lodges.

Quebec City

Walk through the charming old town covered in snow.

  • Activities: Explore Old Quebec, ice skating, and winter festivals.
  • Highlights: Historic architecture, snow-dusted streets, and French-Canadian culture.

Whistler

One of the largest ski resorts in North America.

  • Activities: Skiing, snowboarding, apres-ski, zip-lining.
  • Highlights: World-class slopes and a vibrant village atmosphere.

Ice Hotel (Hôtel de Glace)

Stay in a unique hotel made entirely of ice and snow!

  • Activities: Ice sculptures, ice bar, winter tours.
  • Highlights: Ice rooms, frozen chandeliers, and an unforgettable experience.
