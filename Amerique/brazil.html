Brazil in Summer

Explore the vibrant culture, stunning beaches, and lush nature of Brazil!

Rio de Janeiro

Enjoy the stunning beaches and the lively atmosphere of Brazil’s most famous city.

  • Activities: Beach sports, hiking, samba dancing, sightseeing.
  • Highlights: Christ the Redeemer, Copacabana Beach, Sugarloaf Mountain.

Iguazu Falls

Witness the awe-inspiring Iguazu Falls, one of the largest waterfall systems in the world.

  • Activities: Hiking, boat tours, sightseeing.
  • Highlights: The Devil's Throat, panoramic views, rainforests.

Pantanal

Explore the world’s largest tropical wetland, rich in biodiversity.

  • Activities: Wildlife watching, bird watching, eco-tours.
  • Highlights: Jaguars, capybaras, caimans, and vibrant ecosystems.

Salvador

Experience the colorful culture, vibrant festivals, and beautiful beaches of Salvador.

  • Activities: Exploring historic sites, cultural festivals, beach activities.
  • Highlights: Pelourinho, carnival celebrations, tropical beaches.
