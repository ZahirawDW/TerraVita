Explore Germany Throughout the Seasons

Discover the best places and activities to do in Germany, across all seasons!

Information collected by TerraVita's team

Spring

Berlin

Discover the vibrant city of Berlin during the beautiful spring season.

  • Visit the Brandenburg Gate
  • Explore the Berlin Wall Memorial
  • Relax in Tiergarten Park

Munich

Enjoy the sights of Munich with blooming gardens and parks in spring.

  • Visit Marienplatz Square
  • Explore Nymphenburg Palace
  • Walk through the English Garden

Summer

Lake Constance

Relax and enjoy the sunny shores of Lake Constance during the summer months.

  • Swim and boat on the lake
  • Explore the beautiful lakeside villages
  • Visit Mainau Island

Neuschwanstein Castle

Experience the fairy tale castle amidst the lush greenery of summer.

  • Tour the castle
  • Explore the surrounding forests
  • Visit nearby Hohenschwangau Castle

Fall

Black Forest

Experience the stunning fall foliage and vibrant colors of the Black Forest.

  • Hike the Black Forest Trails
  • Visit Freiburg's Old Town
  • Enjoy local food and drink

Heidelberg

Walk through the charming streets of Heidelberg as the fall colors transform the town.

  • Explore Heidelberg Castle
  • Stroll along the Philosopher's Walk
  • Enjoy the fall foliage by the river

Winter

Garmisch-Partenkirchen

Enjoy skiing and winter activities in the beautiful Bavarian Alps.

  • Skiing and snowboarding
  • Visit the Zugspitze mountain
  • Explore the charming alpine village

Dresden

Discover the beauty of Dresden, with its historic architecture and Christmas markets during winter.

  • Visit the Frauenkirche
  • Explore the Striezelmarkt Christmas market
  • Admire the winter lights and decorations
