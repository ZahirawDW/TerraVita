Greece in Spring

Experience the beauty of Greece in the spring with its iconic landscapes, ancient ruins, and stunning coastlines!

Information collected by TerraVita's team

Athens

Explore the rich history and ancient ruins of Greece's capital city.

  • Activities: Visiting the Acropolis, exploring ancient temples, and strolling through Plaka.
  • Highlights: The Parthenon, ancient ruins, and vibrant street life.

Santorini

Relax on the iconic island of Santorini with its blue-domed churches and stunning sunsets.

  • Activities: Sightseeing, wine tasting, enjoying the sunset, and beach visits.
  • Highlights: Whitewashed buildings, stunning views, and blue-domed churches.

Crete

Discover the largest Greek island with its pristine beaches, ancient ruins, and charming villages.

  • Activities: Hiking in Samaria Gorge, visiting Knossos, and relaxing on beautiful beaches.
  • Highlights: Minoan ruins, stunning landscapes, and Mediterranean cuisine.

Mykonos

Experience the vibrant nightlife and picturesque beaches on this famous Greek island.

  • Activities: Exploring Mykonos Town, partying in beach clubs, and enjoying beach activities.
  • Highlights: White sandy beaches, chic cafes, and lively nightlife.
