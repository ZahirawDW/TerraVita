Netherlands in Spring

Explore the vibrant landscapes and iconic landmarks of the Netherlands during the spring season!

Information collected by TerraVita's team

Amsterdam

Discover the charming canals, tulip gardens, and historical museums.

  • Activities: Canal tours, bike rides, visiting the Anne Frank House and Van Gogh Museum.
  • Highlights: Spring tulips, picturesque canals, and rich cultural heritage.

Keukenhof Gardens

Witness the stunning tulip fields and colorful gardens in full bloom.

  • Activities: Exploring the gardens, taking photographs, and enjoying the vibrant flowers.
  • Highlights: The world's largest flower garden, tulip festivals, and breathtaking floral displays.

Giethoorn

Experience the serene beauty of this village, known as the "Venice of the North."

  • Activities: Boat tours, walking along canals, and exploring peaceful streets.
  • Highlights: Quiet canals, thatched-roof houses, and beautiful bridges.

Zaanse Schans

Step back in time at this iconic windmill village, famous for its traditional Dutch life.

  • Activities: Visiting windmills, cheese factories, and traditional Dutch houses.
  • Highlights: The iconic windmills, picturesque streets, and historical atmosphere.
